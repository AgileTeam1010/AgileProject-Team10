Let's do some division!

Division

is shown using the √∑ symbol

Division is when you take a group of things and share them equally to see how many each one gets.

The division of two whole numbers shows how many times one number can fit into another, or how a total can be split into equal parts.

For example, imagine I have 6 cans of fish, and I want to share them with one of cat friends. To make it fair, I divide the 6 fish cans into 2 equal groups. Each cat gets 3 cans. This can be written as 6 2 = 3 , which is read as "six divided by two equals three".
