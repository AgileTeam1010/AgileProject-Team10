Subtraction

is shown using the - symbol

Subtraction is when you take away a group of things from another group to see how many are left.

The subtraction of one whole number from another shows the difference between the two values.

For example, if we have a group of three cat food cans and we give away two cans, we can subtract to see how many are left. That would leave one cat food can. This observation is expressed as "3 ‚àí 2 = 1", which is read as "three minus two equals one"
