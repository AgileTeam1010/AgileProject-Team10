Let's do some addition!

Addition

is shown using the + symbol

Addition is when you put two groups of things together to see how many you have in total.

The addition of two whole numbers results in the total or sum of those values combined.

For example, if we have two groups of cat food cans, one with three cans and the other with two cans. We can add them together to see how many we have in total, which would be five cat food cans. This observation is expressed as "3 + 2 = 5", which is read as "three plus two equals five".
