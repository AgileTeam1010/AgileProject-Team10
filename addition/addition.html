Addition

is shown using the + symbol

Addition is when you put two groups of things together to see how many you have in total.

The addition of two whole numbers results in the total or sum of those values combined.

For example, if we have two groups of apples, one with two apples and the other with one apple. We can add them together to see how many we have in total, which would be three apples. This observation is expressed as "2 + 1 = 3", which is read as "two plus 1 equals 3".
